About Us Info







At Kare for Kritters, we are dedicated to providing loving homes for animals in need. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets while educating the community about responsible pet ownership. Founded in 2022, we have helped countless animals find their forever homes and continue to work tirelessly to make a difference in their lives.
